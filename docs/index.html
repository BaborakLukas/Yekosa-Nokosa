KO:SA

Defend The Mother. Transform. Survive.

What is KO:SA?

KO:SA is an action/survival game about two brothers who collect shards, and fight off bug-like enemies to protect The Mother - a central entity keeping their world alive.

The Story

The world has been overrun by system bugs - corrupted, insect-like entities that are slowly destroying reality. The last hope is The Mother, a protected core that will give birth to the future savior. The two brothers must hold the line, collect power shards, and keep The Mother alive long enough.

The Mother

Core Features

Local Co-op

Play together as two brothers. One can transform into a weapon while the other wields them - true teamwork!

Wave-Based Combat

Survive increasingly difficult waves of corrupted bugs. Each wave brings stronger enemies and better rewards.

Shard Collection

Collect shards from defeated enemies to upgrade weapons, improve mobility, and enhance The Mother's defenses.

Transformation

Transform into weapon form - you can still jump and dash, but the other player can pick you up as a powerful weapon!

Game Mechanics

Yekosa (Brother A)

Runner / Short-range

  • Stronger at close range
  • Can transform into weapon form
  • In weapon form: jump and dash only
  • Can pick up and use the other brother as a weapon
  • Great for aggressive plays

Nokosa (Brother B)

Gunner / Long-range

  • Stronger at shooting from distance
  • Can transform into weapon form
  • In weapon form: jump and dash only
  • Effective at covering large areas
  • Some combos are stronger when he is the weapon

Core Loop

1Wave starts - enemies spawn
2Players fight - move, transform, carry, shoot
3Enemies drop shards - collect them
4Wave ends - short safe phase
5Buy upgrades - spend shards
6Next wave - harder enemies

Enemies

Basic Bug

Cannon fodder - walks straight to The Mother

Fast Bug

Pressure on players - targets nearest player

Ranged Bug

Denies safe spots - shoots corrupt projectiles

Corruptor (Elite)

Danger for The Mother - tries to drain her HP
